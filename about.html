THE ELECTRICAL & MECHANICAL COMPANY

Antarctic Systems LTD is an electromechanical business founded in 2019 with 9 permanent members of staff.

We have a dedicated team of professional mechanical and electrical engineers to suit your electromechanical service needs within Kenya and East Africa. We have significantly experienced growth and success over the years as a result of meeting out customer’s need with quality products and services with utmost efficiency and courtesy.

We are a team of highly qualified personel with years of experience in the following field:

  • Electrical Installation

    Heavy indutrial and domestic electrical installation

  • Air Conditioning

    Supply, Installation & Maintenance.

  • Mechanical Services

    Generators, Compressors & Ventilation systems.

  • Security & Surveillance

    Supply and Installation of quality security systems.

We take pride in the long-lasting customer relationships built on trust, dedication to serve and a firm commitment to excellence. We are proud of our ability to provide worry-free and guaranteed services.
